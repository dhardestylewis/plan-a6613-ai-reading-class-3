# Source: Vice (2026) — NYC Kills AI Chatbot

**Type**: Technology Journalism
**Date**: February 2026
**URL**: https://www.vice.com/article/nyc-kills-ai-chatbot-mycity/

## Key Content

Mayor Zohran Mamdani announced the shutdown of the MyCity chatbot during a January 28, 2026 press conference on the city's $12 billion budget gap. Mamdani described the chatbot as "functionally unusable."

The chatbot cost over $600,000 to develop and implement under the Adams administration.

The chatbot was officially taken offline in early February 2026. The webpage was updated to state: "The Chatbot beta test has ended. Please go to NYC.gov to find NYC government information."

The termination was positioned as both stopping the spread of misinformation and a budgetary decision to cut inefficient programs.

The chatbot had answered over 58,000 questions by late 2025 with a claimed 95% accuracy rate, but internal audits and independent investigations continued to highlight persistent inconsistencies.
