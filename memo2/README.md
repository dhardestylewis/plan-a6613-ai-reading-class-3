## Service Examined

**NYC MyCity Chatbot** — a Microsoft Azure AI-powered chatbot launched in October 2023 under Mayor Adams' AI Action Plan. The memo covers the chatbot's complete lifecycle: launch, investigative exposure of hallucinated legal advice, 28-month "beta" period, and shutdown in February 2026 by Mayor Mamdani.

## Methodology

Source-first approach developed in Week 7 and refined in Week 9:
1. Download all primary sources before LLM synthesis
2. Annotate each source line-by-line with analytical tags
3. Build synthesis matrix cross-referencing claims across stakeholder positions
4. Draft memo anchored to annotated evidence
5. Conduct claim-by-claim verification audit
