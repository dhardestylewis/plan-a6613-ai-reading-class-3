# Smart Loading Zone Program

We're helping Pittsburgh manage the rapid rise in commercial vehicle activity at the curb.

## More Efficient and Productive Curbs for All

### Zone Locations
Smart Loading Zones are marked by purple signs and curbs and located in Downtown, Squirrel Hill, Shadyside, South Side Flats, Oakland, Lawrenceville, Bloomfield, and other neighborhoods.

### Parking Rates
- Local parking rates reflected
- First 15 mins are FREE
- Max Parking 2 hours
- Max Cost $12 for 2 hours
- Rates posted in each zone

## CurbPass Registration
Drivers register once for a CurbPass to access all Smart Loading Zones.
Registration: https://www.curbpass.io/pittsburgh
Support: https://curbpasshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us

## Key Statistics (from search verification)
- 75 Smart Loading Zones deployed (scaling to 200)
- 40% increase in loading zone turnover
- 23% reduction in average stay duration
- 95% decrease in double-parking
- Cameras mounted on existing streetlight poles
- Cellular-connected cameras with proprietary CV algorithms
- Automated license plate reading and payment collection
- $100K hardware grant from Automotus for initial pilot (2020)
- $3.8M U.S. Department of Energy grant for three-year scale-up
- Pittsburgh Parking Authority (PPA) and DOMI collaboration
