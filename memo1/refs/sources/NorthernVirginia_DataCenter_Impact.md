**Source URLs**: Search-verified data from northernvirginiamag.com, arlnow.com, cardinalnews.org

## Northern Virginia Magazine (2024)
- Northern Virginia is world's largest data center market
- Data center footprint grew by 500% between 2015 and 2023
- Approximately 300 facilities with more under construction
- Current inventory: 4,900 MW; 1,100 MW under construction; ~5,500 new MW planned
- DOE report: region could face over 430 hours of power outages/year by 2030 (vs. 2.4 hours/year currently)
- Coalition to Protect Prince William County and Virginia Data Center Reform Coalition: organized opposition groups
- Complex local dynamics: economic driver (jobs, investment) vs. concerns (power demand, environmental impact, rural quality of life)

## ARLnow (2024)
- Northern Virginia residents reported electricity bill increases exceeding 100% in some months during 2024
- Dominion Energy cited new rates and cold weather as factors
- Residents attribute increases to data center expansion

## Cardinal News (2024)
- Loudoun County collected over $600 million in data center tax revenue in FY2023
- Revenue funded schools and public services without raising residential property tax rates
- Virginia energy demand projected to rise 183% by 2040 due to data centers
