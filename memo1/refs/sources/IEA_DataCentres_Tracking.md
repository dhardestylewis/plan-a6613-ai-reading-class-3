### CO2 Emissions
- Data centres and data transmission networks: ~330 Mt CO2 eq in 2020
- Equivalent to 0.9% of energy-related GHG emissions (0.6% total GHG)
- Emissions grew modestly since 2010 despite rapidly growing demand, thanks to efficiency improvements

### Efficiency Context
- Combined electricity use by Amazon, Microsoft, Google, and Meta more than doubled between 2017 and 2021, rising to ~72 TWh in 2021
- Shift from small enterprise data centres to more efficient cloud and hyperscale data centres
- Large data centres: energy use growing 20-40% annually

### Localized Concentration Examples
- Ireland: data centre electricity use tripled since 2015, now 18% of total national consumption
- Could account for 28% of national demand by 2031
- Denmark: data centre energy use projected to rise 6x by 2030 to ~15% of national electricity
