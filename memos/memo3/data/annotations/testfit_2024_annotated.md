## Application to Memo 3

TestFit is cited as an example of the same spatial redlining logic operating in behavioral form: as hybrid worker demand increases foot traffic and commercial rents in neighborhoods, the rent pressure displaces lower-margin community-serving tenants. The mechanism is behavioral (demand-driven rent pressure) rather than algorithmic (TestFit's optimization parameters), but the spatial outcome is identical — capital flows toward high-income user preferences, and data computationally justifies more of the same.
