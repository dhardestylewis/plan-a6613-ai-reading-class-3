## Application to Memo 3

Vice (2026) is cited in Section III (Looking Ahead) as documentation of the **accountability diffusion failure mode** — the pattern the Neighborhood Time Infrastructure Fund must explicitly design against. The MyCity chatbot failed because responsibility was distributed across Microsoft, OTI, SBS, and the Mayor's office such that no single entity was accountable when the system produced harmful outputs. The fund must have a single accountable implementing office with clear performance metrics.
