## Key Excerpts (verified from web search)

Mission: "Reinvent the development process for the betterment of the world's communities."

TestFit is a real estate feasibility platform that "reduces risk and increases the potential of land through meaningful automation." It uses AI and generative design to automate tasks including drafting unit plans, counting parking stalls, and calculating yield on cost — allowing developers, architects, and urban planners to optimize land use and make data-driven decisions faster.
