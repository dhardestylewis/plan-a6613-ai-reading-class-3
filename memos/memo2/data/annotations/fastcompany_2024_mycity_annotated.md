# Annotation: Fast Company (2024) — MyCity Chatbot Controversy

## Administration Defense [ACCOUNTABILITY]
- Adams admin: testing in "real environment" necessary to "iron out the kinks"
- Treats regulatory compliance as appropriate domain for live experimentation
- Microsoft VP (on Bing chatbot): "You have to actually go out and start to test it with customers" → identical logic

## User Interface Deception [EQUITY]
- Portal updated to add "beta" warning and disclaimer: responses "should not be treated as professional or legal advice"
- But interface still says bot uses "information published by the NYC Department of Small Business Services" and is "trained to provide you official NYC Business information"
- Contradiction: "official NYC Business information" vs. "not professional or legal advice"
- Users with less digital literacy or less familiarity with AI limitations are least equipped to parse this contradiction

## Pilot-to-Scale Problem [FINANCIAL_SUSTAINABILITY]
- First phase (March 2023): child care eligibility
- Second phase (October 2023): business compliance
- Trajectory was expansion, not containment → each phase creates new liability surface
- OTI continued working on system through 2024-2025 before shutdown in 2026

## Worker Impact [CITY_WORKERS]
- Chatbot designed to replace the function of navigating 2,000 pages of municipal code
- In practice: created new work for housing advocates, legal services, and community organizations who had to correct misinformation
- Rosalind Black (Legal Services NYC) had to test the bot after being alerted → unfunded labor of verification shifted to civil society
