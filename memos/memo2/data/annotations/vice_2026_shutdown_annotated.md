# Annotation: Vice (2026) — NYC Kills AI Chatbot

## Shutdown Decision [GOVERNANCE_FAILURE]
- Mayor Zohran Mamdani announced shutdown on January 28, 2026 during press conference on $12B budget gap
- Described chatbot as "functionally unusable" → strongest official condemnation by a sitting mayor of an AI municipal tool
- Shutdown official in early February 2026

## Cost Data [FINANCIAL_SUSTAINABILITY]
- Cost over $600,000 to develop and implement
- Framed as budgetary decision to cut inefficient programs → cost-per-interaction analysis possible: $600K / 58,000 questions = ~$10.34 per question
- Compare: NYC 311 handles ~25 million contacts/year at ~$340M budget → ~$13.60/contact (rough estimate)

## Performance Claims vs. Reality [EFFECTIVENESS]
- OTI claimed 58,000 questions answered by late 2025 with 95% accuracy rate
- "Internal audits and independent investigations continued to highlight persistent inconsistencies" → 95% accuracy claim contested
- For regulatory compliance advice, even 5% error rate is catastrophic: 5% of 58,000 = ~2,900 potentially harmful answers

## Liability Narrative [ACCOUNTABILITY]
