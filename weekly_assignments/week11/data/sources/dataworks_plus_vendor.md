# DataWorks Plus: FACE Plus Facial Recognition

**Source Type:** Corporate / Vendor Marketing  
**Organization:** DataWorks Plus  
**Date:** 2024 Market Overview  

## Product Overview

DataWorks Plus provides state-of-the-art Facial Recognition and multimodal biometric solutions for law enforcement agencies across the globe. Our flagship product, FACE Plus, enables agencies to quickly identify suspects by matching crime scene footage against millions of booking photos and DMV records in seconds.

### The Problem
When a violent crime occurs, every second counts. Investigators often have surveillance stills or cell phone footage but lack a name to attach to the face. Manual review of mugshot books is labor-intensive, slow, and prone to human error.

### The Solution: FACE Plus
Our sophisticated algorithm measures nodal points on a human face—distance between the eyes, width of the nose, and shape of the cheekbones—to generate a rapid mathematical biometric signature. FACE Plus is fully integrated with statewide mugshot repositories, allowing investigators to:
*   Identify unknown suspects immediately.
*   Clear investigative backlogs efficiently.
*   Deploy resources effectively to protect public safety.

### Operational Success
Law enforcement agencies that implement FACE Plus report dramatic increases in case clearance rates. By providing highly accurate investigative leads, our software empowers officers to disrupt criminal networks and resolve critical incidents faster than ever before. DataWorks Plus emphasizes that our system delivers a ranked list of potential matches, allowing human investigators to make the final determination, ensuring a "Human-in-the-Loop" standard for all operational use.
