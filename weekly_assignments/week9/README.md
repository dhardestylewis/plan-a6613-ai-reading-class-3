## Topic
NYC MyCity Business Chatbot: How a generative AI system deployed as an official compliance advisor hallucinated structurally illegal advice, shifting liability risk from the city government onto vulnerable small business owners.

## Methodology
Source-first approach (continuation of Week 7): all 3 primary sources downloaded and annotated before LLM synthesis. Cross-source synthesis matrix constructed to trace every claim in the final document to a specific annotated line in a specific source file.
